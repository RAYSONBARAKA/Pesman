1. Introduction

1.1 Overview

Meosa Limited is a registered company; incorporated under the Companies Act 2015, and the company is Private limited by shares. Meosa has existed for over ten years now, offering top-quality products and services to our esteemed clients in private, Public, and Non-Profit organizations.

1.2 Mission

To be a world-class supplier and service provider of choice in the region and beyond for Socio-Economic Advancement.

1.3 Vision

To achieve profitable growth through the provision of quality goods and services in a timely manner by application of accepted handling methods to the satisfaction of the customers.

1.4 Core Values

  1. Honesty: We strive to provide true information regarding our products to our customers and give undoubtedly feedback.
  2. Fairness: Meosa Limited is committed to handling all clients' complaints in a fair and transparent way to the satisfaction of our customers.
  3. Commitment: We take every complaint that reaches us so seriously and we are committed to taking necessary logical action.
  4. Hard Work: Our team is very focused on making sure that our customers get the best products and service, additionally in case of any complaint that we receive, the team will be determined to work through the process so that the issue is handled immediately.
  5. Confidentiality: Every bit of the information that we receive from the clients will be treated with a lot of secrecy.
